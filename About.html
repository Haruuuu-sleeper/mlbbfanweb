About Us
Mobile Legends: Bang Bang (MLBB) is a globally renowned mobile Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) game developed and published by Moonton, a subsidiary of ByteDance. Since its launch in 2016, MLBB has redefined mobile gaming by offering players a fast-paced, action-packed experience that blends strategy, teamwork, and skill. Designed to deliver seamless gameplay, even on lower-spec devices, MLBB has become a leading title in the mobile gaming industry, uniting millions of players worldwide. At its core, MLBB is built around competitive 5v5 battles where teams work together to defeat their opponents by taking control of key objectives on the battlefield. With an ever-expanding roster of unique heroes, dynamic maps, and innovative game mechanics, the game continuously evolves to provide fresh challenges and thrilling experiences. Whether you prefer playing as a tank, mage, marksman, assassin, fighter, or support, MLBB offers a role for everyone, allowing players to tailor their gameplay to their personal style. Our mission is to create a gaming environment that transcends borders, fostering connections among players from different cultures, languages, and backgrounds. Through engaging gameplay, fair matchmaking, and constant updates, we strive to ensure a balanced and enjoyable experience for players at all skill levels, from beginners to seasoned veterans.
